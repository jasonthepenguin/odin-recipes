The Good Bacon Burger

Description

This is dish is the ultimate cow and pig maxxing meal with an extremely generous protein serving.

Ingredients:

  • 15x slices of streaky american bacon
  • 3x chicken's egg
  • 1x 200g scotch fillet steak
  • 1x Burger bread bun
  • 3x slices of cheddar cheese
  • 3x thin slices of tomato
  • Handful of shredded lettuce

Steps:

  1. Fry the steak and bacon together in the pan for 6 minutes
  2. Fry the eggs for 2 1/2 minutes
  3. Place cheese, tomato and shredded lettuce on bottom of burger bun
  4. Place the finished cooking steak on top, then bacon and finally the eggs
