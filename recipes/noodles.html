The Excellent Noodles

Description

This is the spiciest noodle dish known to any man or pepe. You have been warned.

Ingredients:

  • Ramen noodles
  • Chili Flakes
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Crushed Red Pepper
  • Sichuan Peppercorns
  • Ghost Pepper Powder
  • Sriracha sauce

Steps:

  1. Cook the ramen noodles according to package instructions
  2. Infuse the boiling water with sirarcha sauce
  3. Toss in a handful of each spice ingredient
  4. Once noodles are ready, drain them and then coat every noodle strand with more sriracha sauce
