The Great Pizza

Description

The toppiest topping pizza with the most greasy finger eating experience dish recipe

Ingredients:

  • 20x pepperoni slices
  • 15x black olives chopped
  • 1x green bell pepper chopped thinly
  • Mozzarella cheese grated
  • Bacon diced
  • Marinara sauce

Steps:

  1. Mix all the cooked ingredients into a bowl
  2. Spread marinara sauce evenly on pizza base
  3. Sprinkle by hand all the toppings evenly onto the pizza base
  4. Place on tray and put in the ove at 230 degrees C for 15 minutes
